My C++ Libraries

  • 4th-Order Match Propagation (Please contact me.).

Compiled Qt5 Libraries

DO++ libraries have dependencies with Qt5. Because compiled libraries are not available for Visual Studio in 64bit, I have made my own compiled libraries available here:

  • Qt 5.0.1 compiled with VS2010 64bit (ICU and WebKit disabled) (561 Mb)
  • Qt 5.0.1 compiled with VS2012 64bit (ICU and WebKit disabled) (594 Mb)

Compiled Boost C++ Libraries

I often use Boost (but DO++ does not need it). BoostPro also makes Boost binaries available but only up to VS2010. Just in case, here are my own compiled Boost libraries.

  • Boost 1.46.1 compiled with VS2010 64bit (73.3 Mb)
  • Boost 1.52 compiled with VS2012 64bit (67 Mb)

Don't be fooled by the compressed size of the archives (using LZMA compression), unarchiving them will yield large folders weighing aroung 2.2Gb.
