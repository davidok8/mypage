About me

I have graduated on March 25, 2013 and hold a PhD in Computer Science.

Previously, I was a research assistant advised by Renaud Marlet and Jean-Yves Audibert at IMAGINE, which is part of the Center for Learning and Visual Computing of Ecole Centrale Paris and Ecole des Ponts ParisTech.

Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision and machine learning. In particular, I have focused on:

  • façade analysis
  • feature correspondence
  • pattern detection
  • grammar-based parsing
