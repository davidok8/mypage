Recordings of Guitar Rehearsals at Home

In my spare time, I love playing guitar and inventing music. Unfortunately, my work is taking so much time and I don't have the luxury to devote all my energy for the guitar.

List of Recordings

Here you can find some recorded rehearsals, which I did in order to preserve the technique. Obviously, the playing is SOOOOO far from being perfect and the guitar is recorded with poor means. Maybe, you will enjoy them...

  1. Canción (Chile) - Jorge Cardoso. Fabulous composition using the flamenco tremolo.
    TODO: strenghten the tremolo volume and balance between the voice and the bass. Dynamics can be definitely improved. Stay focused and finish properly.
  2. Milonga - Jorge Cardoso.
    TODO: Redo the second theme and tempo is inconsistent in some parts. Focus on better conveying melancholy and enforce the "tango" atmosphere. Percussive effect too loud.
  3. Improvised Composition 1. First ever recorded improvisation. I was so worried about what to do every two seconds. All the same, despite being very simple, I think that the playing sounds not too bad.
    TODO: Re-record with a better microphone. Pursue ideas and synthesize themes.
  4. Improvised Composition 2. Borrows some ideas as in the first composition but played with more confidence.

Other recordings will be coming later when time allows it. I am learning (at an irregular basis though) other pieces to absorb new ideas. Existing ones will be replaced when I play them better: like good ol' wine, musicianship ages well over the years.
